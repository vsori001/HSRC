Advent Silent Saturday
Presented by Lylia Dange and Contemplative Outreach Team

Saturday, December 6 9:00am - 12:00pm
(Arrival 8:30am - 9:00am)

Please join us for a quiet morning of Centering Prayer and silence. The day is open to beginners as well as those experienced in contemplative prayer or silent meditation. The day provides time for communal prayer, a contemplative walk, private journaling and reflection and an opportunity to enjoy the beautiful grounds of Holy Spirit Retreat Center. All are welcome. A brief introduction to Centering Prayer will be offered for those new to the practice.

Please register by November 28, 2014.

Free-will offering.
