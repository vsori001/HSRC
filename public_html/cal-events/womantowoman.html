Woman To Woman
Presented by Josie Broehm, MFT

Thursdays 10:00am - 1:00pm

Woman of all ages and traditions gather monthly to nurture their spiritual identity and to reflect on themes that challenge us as women in today’s world. Do come and join the conversation, as your schedule permits, and meet other women eager to explore new possibilities at every age.

We recommend that you bring your journals/writing materials; dress comfortably for simple movement experiences.

Josie Broehm is a licensed psychotherapist and spiritual director in private practice. She works with dreams and assists individuals in creating rituals for life’s sacred moments.

Fee: $45 with lunch, $35 without lunch
