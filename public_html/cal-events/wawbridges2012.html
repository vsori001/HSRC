Women at the Well/Bridges

Thursdays: January 19, February 16, March 15, April 19, May 17
7:00pm - 9:00pm
Sr. Chris Machado, SSS

Our evening gathering continues to meet with a small but devoted group of women. As with our daytime group, we are always open to new members. In September of 2009 we began the Bridges to Contemplative Living series, which is based partly on the writings of Thomas Merton. We are just beginning book three for our Bridges gatherings and are loving the program! Bridges fosters a deeper, more contemplative life by encouraging a proper relationship to God, self, others, and nature. Our gatherings also include prayer, reflection time, support, contemplative dialogue, and Centering Prayer. It's a wonderful and restful way to end the day. Come explore the riches of living life on a deeper and more meaningful level!

Registration not required.
Suggested donation of $5 per session.
