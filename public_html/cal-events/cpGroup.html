Centering Prayer is a silent meditation practice intended to deepen one’s relationship with God. The group sessions include time for communal Centering Prayer, a contemplative walk, input from a Spiritual Journey DVD or book by Thomas Keating and group sharing. If you are new to the practice we highly recommend the Centering Prayer Introductory Workshop. For more information, please call Linda Snow, CSJ or Pat Nelson, CSJ at 818-784-4515 x317.
