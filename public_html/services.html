The Three Treasures: Healing of Mind-Body-Spirit

Sr. Deborah Lorentz, Sister of Social Service, is a licensed acupuncturist in the State of California as well as a Diplomate in Acupuncture accredited by the NCCAOM. She has been in practice for ten years. Seeing the need for healing of the mind, body, and spirit, she offers a Christian healing practice that assists those who are searching for God to find physical and spiritual relief and relaxation.

Holy Spirit Retreat is please to offer her services. Currently she sees patients by appointment only. She is willing to offer acupuncture during both hosted and sponsored events if requested. She also sees clients at her clinic. Please contact Sr. Deborah Lorentz for more information or to set up an appointment call (213) 509 - 8610 or (818) 784 - 4515 ext. #306.
